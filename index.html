About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Data Science at Queensland University of Technology, my research interest involves hierarchical forecasting, federated learning, and machine learning applications in renewable energy forecasting and control. Previously, I obtained a Master's degree in Data Science at The University of Queensland (UQ), 2025, and a Bachelor's degree in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics at Nanjing Tech University, China, 2022.

My experience involves Time Series Forecasting, Stock Price Prediction, Ocular Diseases Recognition and a bit of Optimisation and High-dimensional Data Analysis, e.g., Swarm Optimisation Algorithm (SOA) and Late-onset Alzheimer's disease. I am also passionate about using theories from mathematics, statistics, and data science combined with domain knowledge to solve practical problems across industries.

I played for the basketball team of School of Mathematics and Physics at NJTech Uni for 4 years, and made many close friends there. Sport shaped my personalities. Please feel free to drop me an email with any purpose, i.e. greeting, discussion, consultation, questions or chat.
